STARBUCKS DRINKS

Six ways to sip the holidays
Celebrate the return of your seasonal favorites. Chestnut Praline Latte, Peppermint Mocha, Caramel Brulée Latte, and Egg Nog Latte are back and can
be enjoyed hot or cold. Try our HolidaySpice Flat White, where warm spices are added to our signature flat white. And new this year is Spiced Sweet
Cream Cold Brew, where Nariño 70 Cold Brew is sweetened with holiday spices & finished with our house-made sweet cream. Be
sure to enjoy them before the holidays are over.

FEATURED OFFERINGS

Spiced Sweet Cream Cold Brew
Our Nariño 70 cold brew coffee (slow-steeped for a super smooth
flavor) gets dressed up for the holidays with a dash of spice and a
float of house-made sweet cream.


Holiday Spice Flat White
Smooth ristretto shots of Christmas Blend Espresso Roast and
perfectly steamed micro-foam are infused with warm holiday
spices to create a festive version of an espresso classic.


Peppermint Mocha
A holiday classic made with our signature espresso, steamed milk,
sweet mocha sauce and peppermint-flavored syrup. It's all topped
off with whipped cream and dark chocolate curls.
